Welcome to TimChist Adventures

TimChist Adventures is a privately owned tourism business registered in Kenya in 2017.
At TimChist we make travel easy and tours memorable.

We not only offer planned tour experiences, we also allow you to customize your own travel by exposing and informing you about different travel experiences and services being offered in different destinations around the country.
